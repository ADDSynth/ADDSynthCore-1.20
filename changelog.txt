
Version 1.0 - TBD
NEW:
  Added Auto Jukebox.
    Can hold up to 27 Music Discs. Plays them in order or shuffled. Can also play Music Discs from other mods.
  Added simple Backpacks:
    Holds up to 27 items, and of course, you cannot insert a Backpack into another Backpack. Use Anvil to rename.
  Added Personal Beacon:
    Requires Curios. Place in Charm slot to apply Glowing Effect, allowing other players to see you through walls.
  Added Watering Can:
    Right-click water to fill. Up to 64 uses. Use to bonemeal things and also hydrates sponge.
  Added Hedge Trimmers:
    Left-click any simple plant that breaks in one hit and this will destroy all plants in a large radius.
  Added Seashells.
  Added the /addsynthcore map_colors Command. Spawns blocks of each MapColor at the highest point in the world.
   - Another command meant to help developers.
  Added more mods to detect if "Print Mod Detection Results" is turned on in the config:
   - Now detects GregTech CEu Modern, Architectury, FastWorkbench, EvilCraft, Common Capabilities,
     Oh My Gourd, Xaero's World Map, Xaero's Minimap, GunpowderLib, Chance Cubes, GraveStone Mod,
     TrashSlot, Steve's Carts, Configure, AppleSkin, Bookshelf, Portal Gun Mod, and Bad Packets.
CHANGED:
  Removed the top ingredient in Trophy crafting recipes so all trophies now only require 5 of their
    main ingredient instead of 6.
  Debug configuration settings have been moved to their own configuration file.
REMOVED:
  Removed Trophy Base item.



For older version history, please see Overpowered Technology changelog BEFORE version 6.
